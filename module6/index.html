The city of Vantaa


Vantaa is the city where i've lived my whole life even though i was born in Espoo


Vantaa is a city in Finland. It is located to the north of the capital, Helsinki, in southern Uusimaa. The population of Vantaa is approximately 251,000. It is the 4th most populous municipality in Finland. Vantaa is part of the Helsinki Metropolitan Area, which has approximately 1.61 million inhabitants.


According to the 2025 disturbance index used by the Finnish Police, Vantaa is currently the most dangerous city of Finland, and Myyrmäki its most dangerous district.
